About TreeSeek

sometimes when i'm walking, i see a tree i like. i'm not great at identifying trees by sight, but i'd like to know what i'm looking at. this web application was built to help with that.

the tree data comes from city open data sites. right now there's just toronto, ottawa, and montreal because those are some cities i sometimes find myself walking and wondering about trees.

mapping by Leaflet. tile hosting by OpenFreeMap.
